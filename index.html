Pints & Shells

A brewery locater using Google Maps API. Built using React, Node, Express, MongoDB.

Pints & Shells was created for users who are fans of craft beer and love to attend breweries to learn about the art of brewing or just to enjoy beers with friends. Our search at the moment only includes the immediate Austin area. We plan to increase user area through the majority of Texas by the start of 2019, and hope to fully operational in the United States by spring 2019!

  • Date: November 2018

Open Road

A platform for the inner traveler in all of us.

Have you ever wanted to take a road trip but not sure where to go? Open Road helps users to view trips created by other users in order to find new ideas for trips or you can create a trip of your own. Open Road was created using Node and Express.

  • Date: October 2018

Epoch

An E-commerce platform desgined for a flawless user experience while maintaining the highest quality of luxury watches

Watches are timeless. The pure elegance of a luxury watch can set the whole mood. Epoch was created as an E-commerce template for a fully operational online store. In the process of studying Stripe payment API to incorporate it into my website.

  • Date: December 2018
